About Me

I’m Jaiden McGee, also known as Active2Fit. Growing up, I was always skinny. I wanted to change, so I tried everything to gain weight—protein powders, eating nonstop—you name it. I thought it would get me in shape, but instead it left me skinny-fat. The frustration only grew stronger. I was in a worse position than when I started. My confidence dropped. I even began blaming my genetics. But then I discovered the real issue. See, a lot of guys will tell you to “just eat more.” The truth is, eating everything in sight does more harm than good. Real progress comes from eating with strategy, not just quantity. That simple shift changed everything for me. Within weeks, I started seeing results, and with those results came confidence. Fast forward to today—I’ve built a physique I’m truly proud of. Not just because of how it looks, but because I know I built it from the ground up. And if I can do that, I can do anything. Now, I’m on a mission: to help as many men as possible build a strong, confident physique that not only transforms their body, but also their belief in what they’re capable of achieving..
